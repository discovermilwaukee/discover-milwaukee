NEIGHBORHOOD GUIDE

Riverwest

Milwaukee's bohemian heart. Artists, activists, eccentrics, and everyone in between call this proudly weird neighborhood home.

JUMP TO

Where to Eat|Things to Do|Nightlife

The Vibe

Riverwest is Milwaukee's most unapologetically itself neighborhood. Bordered by the Milwaukee River and home to a diverse mix of artists, students, longtime residents, and free spirits, it's the kind of place where community gardens thrive, murals cover the walls, and nobody's trying too hard to be cool. The annual Riverwest 24 bike race captures the spirit perfectly: grassroots, chaotic, inclusive, and deeply local. This isn't a neighborhood for tourists—it's a neighborhood for people.

Where to Eat & Drink

Unpretentious, affordable, and full of character—dining in Riverwest reflects the neighborhood.

Fuel Cafe

$

Cafe/Brunch

The heart and soul of Riverwest. This vegetarian-friendly cafe has been fueling the neighborhood's artists, activists, and eccentrics since 1993. Strong coffee, hearty breakfast, and a bulletin board full of local happenings.

MUST TRY

Any breakfast burrito, French toast, strong coffee

INSIDER TIP

Cash only. Weekend mornings can have a wait, but the patio is worth it.

818 E Center St, Milwaukee, WI 53212

Comet Cafe

$

American Comfort

A Milwaukee institution serving elevated comfort food with a punk rock attitude. Famous for meat loaf, vegan options, and a Bloody Mary bar that's become a Sunday tradition. The tattoo-friendly vibe is quintessentially Riverwest-adjacent.

MUST TRY

Meat loaf, bacon-wrapped dates, Sunday Bloody Mary bar

INSIDER TIP

The Bloody Mary bar on Sundays is legendary—build your own masterpiece.

1947 N Farwell Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Uptowner

$

Bar/Pub Food

A true Riverwest dive bar serving cheap drinks and surprisingly good food. The jukebox is excellent, the crowd is eclectic, and the cheeseburgers are some of the best in the city.

MUST TRY

Cheeseburger, cheap beer, jukebox picks

INSIDER TIP

Cash only, ATM inside. The burger is seriously underrated.

1032 E Center St, Milwaukee, WI 53212

Riverwest Co-op

$

Grocery/Deli

Member-owned cooperative grocery with a small deli serving fresh, local, and organic options. A perfect stop for a quick lunch or to stock up on specialty items.

MUST TRY

Deli sandwiches, local produce, bulk goods

INSIDER TIP

Non-members can shop too—just a small markup. Check the events board.

733 E Clarke St, Milwaukee, WI 53212

Brewers Hill Eatery

$$

American/Brunch

Neighborhood spot on the edge of Riverwest/Brewers Hill serving solid American fare with great brunch options. The patio is lovely in summer.

MUST TRY

Brunch dishes, burgers, craft beer selection

INSIDER TIP

Quieter than East Side spots but just as good.

1060 E Pleasant St, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Foundation

$$

Tiki Bar/Pizza

A tiki bar in Riverwest serving excellent Neapolitan-style pizza? It shouldn't work, but it absolutely does. Strong tropical drinks, creative pies, and a unique vibe make this a neighborhood gem.

MUST TRY

Tiki cocktails, pizza specials, the atmosphere

INSIDER TIP

The tiki drinks are strong—pace yourself.

2718 N Bremen St, Milwaukee, WI 53212

Things to Do

Live music, local art, outdoor spaces, and community events define the Riverwest experience.

Riverwest 24 (Annual Event)

Cycling Event

The legendary 24-hour bike race through Riverwest streets. Teams race around the clock in this chaotic, community-driven celebration of cycling and neighborhood pride.

July eventCommunity spirit24-hour race

Music Venue/Bar

Intimate live music venue hosting local and touring acts. Folk, blues, americana, and everything in between. One of the best small music rooms in the city.

Nightlife

Dive bars, music venues, and places where everyone knows your name.

Vibe: Music-focused, intimate, authentic

Tiki Bar
