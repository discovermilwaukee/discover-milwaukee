Best Cheap Eats in Milwaukee

Best Overall Cheap Eats

The spots that define budget dining in Milwaukee. These restaurants deliver exceptional value without compromising on flavor.

Conejito's Place

$3-8Walker's PointNo-Frills Authentic Mexican

Voted #1 for Cheap Eats by Shepherd Express, Conejito's has been a Milwaukee institution for decades. Nothing on the menu exceeds $8, and the food is served on paper plates in a no-frills atmosphere. The chicken mole is legendary, and the portions are generous enough to leave you stuffed without emptying your wallet.

MUST TRY

Chicken Mole, Tacos, Enchiladas

BEST FOR

Budget seekers, authentic Mexican, cash-only dining

Budget tip: Full meal with drink under $10

Insider tip: Cash only. Come hungry because the portions are massive. The wait can be long on weekends, but it moves fast.

539 W Virginia St, Milwaukee, WI 53204

Oscar's Pub & Grill

$8-14Mitchell ParkClassic Pub Fare

Oscar's has earned a devoted following for its outstanding burgers and incredible value. The pub atmosphere is welcoming, and the food consistently exceeds expectations for the price. Their $1 taco specials are legendary, and the craft beer selection pairs perfectly with their menu.

MUST TRY

Oscar's Burger, $1 Tacos, Sweet Potato Fries

BEST FOR

Burger lovers, taco night deals, neighborhood pub vibes

Budget tip: Load up on $1 taco nights for an ultra-cheap meal

Insider tip: Check their social media for $1 taco nights. The Pierce Street location is the original and has the best vibe.

1712 W Pierce St, Milwaukee, WI 53204

Transfer Pizzeria Cafe

$7-15Historic Mitchell StreetArtisan Pizza & Cafe

Transfer brings artisan-quality pizza to Milwaukee's South Side at prices that won't break the bank. Their weekday lunch deal of a one-topping pizza and soda for $6.95 is one of the best values in the city. The space is warm and inviting with exposed brick and local art.

MUST TRY

Margherita Pizza, Lunch Special, Vegan Options

BEST FOR

Pizza lovers, lunch deals, vegan-friendly options

Budget tip: Lunch special under $7 with a drink

Insider tip: The weekday lunch special (11am-3pm) is unbeatable: one-topping pizza plus soda for $6.95.

101 W Mitchell St, Milwaukee, WI 53204

Camino

$5-14Walker's PointAmerican Craft Beer & Eats

Camino is Walker's Point's go-to spot for affordable pub food with a Latin twist. Their $5 burger Monday special features a half-pound beef patty with two slices of American cheese and fried onions on a brioche bun. The craft beer selection is excellent and reasonably priced.

MUST TRY

$5 Monday Burger, Wednesday Burger Time, Wings

BEST FOR

Burger deals, craft beer fans, Walker's Point crowds

Budget tip: $5 burger on Mondays is one of the city's best deals

Insider tip: Monday $5 burgers and Wednesday unlimited burger toppings for $10 are the best deals. Great patio in summer.

434 S 2nd St, Milwaukee, WI 53204

Tacos & Taquerias

Milwaukee's South Side serves some of the best affordable tacos in the Midwest. Authentic flavors, rock-bottom prices.

Taqueria El Cabrito

$2-8Walker's PointTraditional Mexican & Birria

This cozy brick-walled diner is beloved for its slow-cooked goat meat (birria), which is the star of the menu. You can get a full meal with two tacos and a quesadilla for under $8, making it one of the best values on Milwaukee's South Side. The homemade tortillas and salsas are exceptional.

MUST TRY

Birria Tacos, Quesadilla, Horchata

BEST FOR

Birria lovers, authentic Mexican, best bang for buck

Budget tip: Two tacos and quesadilla under $8

Insider tip: The birria is the specialty here. Order it in tacos or as a plate. The homemade salsas range from mild to fiery.

1100 S 11th St, Milwaukee, WI 53204

Taqueria Buenavista

$2-10West MilwaukeeAuthentic Street Tacos

With multiple locations and food trucks around the city, Taqueria Buenavista has become a Milwaukee institution for affordable authentic Mexican food. Tacos are just $2-3 each, served on warm corn tortillas with perfect seasoning. The atmosphere is unpretentious and family-friendly.

MUST TRY

Street Tacos, Tortas, Burritos

BEST FOR

Authentic street tacos, family dining, multiple locations

Budget tip: Tacos $2-3 each, full meal under $10

Insider tip: They have food trucks at Chase & Oklahoma, 108th & National, and 92nd & Greenfield. The trucks often have shorter lines.

6000 W Burnham St, Milwaukee, WI 53219

Mazorca Tacos

$4-12Walker's Point (Zocalo Food Park)Award-Winning Tacos

Located in the Zocalo Food Park, Mazorca has earned editor's pick status for their handmade tortillas grilled for extra crispness. The tacos, tortas, and burritos are made with exceptional care and quality ingredients. The food park setting makes it a fun outdoor dining experience.

MUST TRY

Al Pastor Tacos, Carnitas, Handmade Tortillas

BEST FOR

Handmade tortillas, food park vibes, quality over quantity

Budget tip: Three tacos make a filling meal for under $15

Insider tip: The handmade tortillas are what set Mazorca apart. Get them grilled for extra crispiness. Open Wed-Sun only.

636 S 6th St, Milwaukee, WI 53204

Guanajuato Mexican Restaurant

$8-14Bay ViewFamily-Style Mexican

Serving authentic Mexican cuisine for over 30 years, Guanajuato is Milwaukee's reader-voted best overall taco spot. The warm, welcoming atmosphere and generous portions have made it a Bay View staple. Everything from the salsas to the tortillas is made fresh daily.

MUST TRY

Tacos, Enchiladas, Combination Plates

BEST FOR

Family dining, authentic flavors, Bay View locals

Budget tip: Lunch specials under $10

Insider tip: The lunch specials are an even better value. Ask for extra chips and salsa - they're happy to oblige.

2317 S Howell Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53207

BelAir Cantina

$2-14Multiple LocationsModern Mexican & Tacos

BelAir Cantina brings a modern twist to Mexican cuisine with creative tacos and an extensive tequila selection. Their Taco Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday deals offer $2 select tacos, making it one of the best recurring specials in the city. Multiple locations make it accessible from anywhere.

MUST TRY

$2 Select Tacos, Baja Fish Taco, Margaritas

BEST FOR

Taco deals, modern atmosphere, date nights on a budget

Budget tip: $2 tacos Tue-Thu can get you stuffed for under $10

Insider tip: $2 select tacos Tuesday-Thursday. Also locations on Downer Ave and in Wauwatosa.

1935 N Water St, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Pizza Deals

From creative slices to classic Milwaukee pies, pizza on a budget has never been better.

Ian's Pizza

$3-5/sliceMultiple LocationsCreative Slices & Classics

Ian's Pizza brings affordable, craft-quality pizza to the people with creative slice options that change daily. Famous for inventions like Mac & Cheese Pizza, they offer slices ranging from $3-4.75. Three Milwaukee locations (North Ave, Downtown, Story Hill) make it accessible citywide.

MUST TRY

Mac & Cheese Pizza, Daily Specials, Classic Cheese

BEST FOR

Creative slices, late night, multiple locations

Budget tip: Two slices make a meal for under $10

Insider tip: Check their social media for daily slice specials. The late-night hours make it perfect for after-bar cravings.

2035 E North Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Zaffiro's Pizza

$10-15Lower East SideMilwaukee Classic Thin Crust

A Milwaukee institution since 1954, Zaffiro's serves legendary thin-crust pizza that has earned devoted fans for generations. Their large two-topping pizza for $15 (dine-in only) is one of the best pizza deals in the city. The no-frills atmosphere keeps the focus on the food.

MUST TRY

Classic Thin Crust, Two-Topping Large, Pitcher of Beer

BEST FOR

Milwaukee tradition, thin crust lovers, dine-in deals

Budget tip: Large two-topping pizza $15 feeds two people

Insider tip: The $15 large two-topping is dine-in only. Split with a friend and add a pitcher for a cheap night out.

1724 N Farwell Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Pizza Man

$10-15Downer Ave / WauwatosaMilwaukee Institution

Pizza Man has been a Milwaukee favorite for decades, known for quality ingredients and a welcoming atmosphere. Their lunch special offers a 12-inch one-topping pizza with a soda for $13.95, and happy hour brings discounted apps and drinks.

MUST TRY

Lunch Special, Any Specialty Pizza, Calzones

BEST FOR

Lunch deals, East Side dining, happy hour

Budget tip: Lunch special under $14 with a drink

Insider tip: Lunch special (11am-3pm): 12" one-topping pizza + soda for $13.95. Happy hour 3-6pm has great deals.

2597 N Downer Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53211

Affordable Ethnic Food

Global flavors at budget-friendly prices. Milwaukee's diverse food scene delivers world cuisine without the world prices.

Vientiane Noodle Shop

$8-14Silver CityLaotian & Thai

This family-owned gem has been serving authentic Laotian cuisine for two decades. Their pho, pad Thai, and Southeast Asian specialties are made with care and served in generous portions. The spring roll dipping sauce alone is worth the visit.

MUST TRY

Pho, Pad See Ew, Spring Rolls, Pad Thai

BEST FOR

Authentic Southeast Asian, pho lovers, hidden gem

Budget tip: Large bowl of pho under $12 is a full meal

Insider tip: The spring roll sauce is legendary. Try the pad see ew if you want something different from typical Thai.

3422 W National Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53215

Casablanca

$8-14Brady StreetMiddle Eastern

A Brady Street institution since 1988, Casablanca serves authentic Middle Eastern fare at reasonable prices. The spicy chicken shawarma with fries for $11 is a bargain, and the atmosphere transforms from restaurant to hookah lounge after dark. Open until 2 AM.

MUST TRY

Chicken Shawarma, Lamb Shank Couscous, Combination Appetizer

BEST FOR

Middle Eastern, late night, Brady Street vibe

Budget tip: Shawarma plate with fries $11

Insider tip: The combination appetizer is perfect for sharing. Late-night menu available until 2 AM.

728 E Brady St, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Apollo Cafe

$10-14Brady StreetGreek & Mediterranean

Apollo Cafe has been serving Greek favorites on Brady Street for years. Their gyros at $12.50 are packed with meat and served with fresh pita and tzatziki. The casual atmosphere and consistent quality have earned them a loyal following.

MUST TRY

Gyros, Greek Salad, Spanakopita

BEST FOR

Greek food, Brady Street, gyro lovers

Budget tip: Gyro meal under $13

Insider tip: The gyro meat is sliced fresh from the spit. Pair it with a Greek salad for a complete meal under $15.

1310 E Brady St, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Doner Kebab

$10-14North SideGerman-Style Doner

Milwaukee's first dedicated doner kebab shop opened in late 2025, bringing authentic German-style doner to the city. The doner sandwiches are made with traditional preparation and served with fresh vegetables and house sauces. A unique addition to Milwaukee's ethnic food scene.

MUST TRY

Doner Kebab Sandwich, Fries, House Sauces

BEST FOR

German street food, unique eats, North Side dining

Budget tip: Doner sandwich meal under $14

Insider tip: Opened by Ray and Elma Ramic in 2025. Try the traditional doner with all the fixings for the full experience.

138 E Capitol Dr, Milwaukee, WI 53212

Pho Saigon

$10-14West AllisVietnamese Pho & Noodles

Pho Saigon serves steaming bowls of Vietnamese pho and noodle dishes that warm the soul without emptying your wallet. The rich, aromatic broth is simmered for hours, and the portions are generous. A perfect spot for a filling, affordable meal.

MUST TRY

Pho with Rare Steak, Banh Mi, Vietnamese Coffee

BEST FOR

Pho lovers, cold day comfort food, Vietnamese cuisine

Budget tip: Large pho under $14 can feed two

Insider tip: The large pho is enormous - perfect for sharing or taking leftovers home. Add extra sriracha and hoisin to taste.

10534 W Greenfield Ave, West Allis, WI 53214

Opened in 1973 in the iconic Mackie Building, Swingin' Door Exchange is a downtown institution known for generous portions and reasonable prices. The menu features homemade soups, sandwiches, steaks, and daily features. The historic saloon atmosphere is quintessentially Milwaukee.

MUST TRY

BEST FOR

Downtown lunch, historic atmosphere, generous portions

Carnitas Don Lucho

$8-15Lincoln VillageWeekend Carnitas Specialist

This weekend-only spot specializes in traditional Mexican carnitas prepared the old-fashioned way. The tender, flavorful pork is cooked in copper pots and served by the pound. Worth waking up early for - they open at 4 AM and often sell out by afternoon.

MUST TRY

Carnitas by the Pound, Tacos, Tortas

BEST FOR

Authentic carnitas, early birds, weekend tradition

Budget tip: Carnitas by the pound to share is best value

Insider tip: Opens at 4 AM Friday-Sunday and sells out fast. Get there early for the best selection. Weekend only!

565 W Lincoln Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53207

Kopp's Frozen Custard

$5-12Glendale / Greenfield / BrookfieldMilwaukee Custard & Burgers

Founded in 1950, Kopp's is a Milwaukee institution for frozen custard and jumbo burgers. The daily flavor of custard draws devoted fans who track their favorites, and the burgers are made fresh with quality ingredients. Three locations serve the greater Milwaukee area.

MUST TRY

Jumbo Burger, Daily Custard Flavor, Cheese Curds

BEST FOR

Milwaukee tradition, frozen custard, family outings

Budget tip: Burger and small custard under $12

Insider tip: Check their website for the daily custard flavor. The jumbo burger is massive - consider the regular size.

5373 N Port Washington Rd, Glendale, WI 53217

Ono Kine Grindz

$12-15WauwatosaHawaiian Plate Lunch

Milwaukee's go-to spot for authentic Hawaiian food, Ono Kine Grindz serves generous plate lunches that transport you to the islands. The kalua pork, chicken katsu, and spam musubi are all prepared traditionally. Also a market for Hawaiian products and Kona coffee.

MUST TRY

Kalua Pork Plate, Chicken Katsu, Spam Musubi

BEST FOR

Hawaiian food, unique cuisine, island flavors

Budget tip: Plate lunch under $15 is extremely filling

Insider tip: The plate lunches come with two scoops of rice and mac salad - very filling. Limited hours, check before going.

7215 W North Ave, Wauwatosa, WI 53213

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the cheapest place to eat in Milwaukee?

Conejito's Place in Walker's Point is consistently voted Milwaukee's best cheap eats, with nothing on the menu over $8. Taqueria El Cabrito offers two tacos and a quesadilla for under $8. For deals, Camino has $5 burgers on Mondays, and BelAir Cantina offers $2 tacos Tuesday through Thursday.

Where can I find the best cheap tacos in Milwaukee?

Milwaukee's South Side is taco heaven. Taqueria El Cabrito serves incredible birria tacos under $3 each. Taqueria Buenavista has multiple locations with $2-3 street tacos. Mazorca Tacos at Zocalo Food Park offers award-winning handmade tortilla tacos. BelAir Cantina's $2 taco specials run Tuesday through Thursday.

What are the best pizza deals in Milwaukee?

Transfer Pizzeria offers a weekday lunch deal: one-topping pizza and soda for $6.95. Ian's Pizza sells creative slices for $3-4.75 at three locations. Zaffiro's has a large two-topping pizza for $15 dine-in. Pizza Man's lunch special is a 12-inch pizza with soda for $13.95.

Where can I find cheap ethnic food in Milwaukee?

Vientiane Noodle Shop serves large bowls of pho under $14. Casablanca on Brady Street has shawarma plates for $11. Apollo Cafe offers gyros for $12.50. Doner Kebab on Capitol Drive serves authentic German-style doner under $14. Pho Saigon in West Allis has filling Vietnamese dishes at great prices.

What are the best daily food specials in Milwaukee?

Monday: Camino $5 burgers. Tuesday-Thursday: BelAir Cantina $2 select tacos. Wednesday: Camino unlimited burger toppings for $10. Many spots offer lunch specials: Transfer ($6.95 pizza combo), Pizza Man ($13.95 pizza combo). Check Oscar's Pub for $1 taco night specials.
