Best Rooftop Bars in Milwaukee

Elevated drinking with skyline views. Milwaukee's best rooftops for cocktails, craft beer, and unforgettable sunsets.

Best Views

The rooftops where the view is as impressive as the drinks.

The Outsider

$$$Third Ward

Upscale, trendy, hotel chic

Nine floors up at the Kimpton Journeyman Hotel, The Outsider is Milwaukee's most sophisticated rooftop experience. Six unique indoor and outdoor areas, swanky cocktail lounge vibes, and stunning skyline views year-round. This is where the views meet the cocktails.

THE VIEW

Downtown skyline, 360° views

BEST TIME

Sunset for golden hour views

RESERVATIONS

Recommended, especially weekends

INSIDER TIP

Make a reservation for sunset. The heated areas make this a year-round option.

Kimpton Journeyman Hotel, 310 E Chicago St, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Skyline Bar + Lounge

$$$Downtown

Modern, artistic, sophisticated

Perched atop Nō Studios, Skyline Bar + Lounge delivers unobstructed 360° views of the city. The modern, glass-enclosed space opens to two outdoor patios. It's equal parts art gallery and rooftop bar.

THE VIEW

360° unobstructed city views

BEST TIME

Late afternoon through sunset

RESERVATIONS

Recommended for groups

INSIDER TIP

Check out the studios and art spaces before heading up for drinks.

Nō Studios, 1037 W McKinley Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53205

Café Benelux / Lux Skyhaus

$$-$$$Third Ward

European al fresco, Belgian-inspired

Milwaukee's first rooftop with a retractable enclosure. The Lux Skyhaus transforms from open-air European vibes on beautiful days to a heated, enclosed space when weather demands. Belgian beer selection is excellent.

THE VIEW

Third Ward streetscape, downtown skyline

BEST TIME

Summer afternoons, any season with the enclosure

RESERVATIONS

Recommended, especially weekends

INSIDER TIP

The Belgian beer list is extensive—ask for recommendations. Brunch is popular too.

346 N Broadway, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Deer District / Game Day

Perfect for pre-game drinks or watching the Deer District energy from above.

Uncle Buck's

$$Deer District

Sports bar, lively, energetic

The ultimate game-day rooftop next to Fiserv Forum. Two patios stretch across the rooftop, built around a fire bar. Views of the skyline and the Deer District crowd below make this perfect for Bucks or Brewers game days.

THE VIEW

Deer District, downtown skyline

BEST TIME

Game days, summer evenings

RESERVATIONS

Walk-in friendly, but early arrival recommended for games

INSIDER TIP

Arrive early on game days—it fills up fast. The fire bar is great for cooler evenings.

1120 N Phillips Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53203

Pilot Project

$$Pabst Brewery District

Craft beer focused, casual, brewery vibes

Brewery incubator, restaurant, and rooftop bar in one vibrant venue. The spacious rooftop terrace offers comfy lounge seating and bar spots, covered and open-air areas, with lovely views over downtown and the Deer District.

THE VIEW

Downtown skyline, Deer District

BEST TIME

Pre-game drinks, summer afternoons

RESERVATIONS

Usually walk-in friendly

INSIDER TIP

Try the rotating guest brewery taps—each one brings something different.

1128 N 9th St, Milwaukee, WI 53233

Cocktail-Focused

When the drinks matter as much as the views.

The Aviary

$$-$$$Downtown

Craft cocktails, trendy, modern

Central Standard Crafthouse's crown jewel. The open-air rooftop terrace called The Aviary is spacious and modern with plenty of bar-style seating surrounded by fantastic skyline views. Craft cocktails are the star.

THE VIEW

Milwaukee skyline

BEST TIME

Evening cocktail hour

RESERVATIONS

Helpful for groups

INSIDER TIP

The cocktail program is serious—don't be afraid to ask the bartenders for recommendations.

Central Standard Crafthouse, 320 E Clybourn St, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Tiki bar, tropical, escapist

Tiki-inspired rooftop escape with creative cocktails like 'Get Lost' and 'Ocean Eyes.' The tropical vibes make you forget you're in the Midwest. Perfect for escaping the everyday.

THE VIEW

Urban rooftop

BEST TIME

Summer evenings

RESERVATIONS

Recommended on weekends

INSIDER TIP

Embrace the tiki theme—order something with rum and a tiny umbrella.

Walker's Point, Milwaukee

Casual Vibes

Camp Bar Third Ward

$$Third Ward

Casual, camping-themed, dog-friendly

A two-story camping-themed bar with a rooftop patio that's more casual than most. Think fire pits, cozy seating, and a laid-back vibe. Dog-friendly too.

THE VIEW

Third Ward neighborhood

BEST TIME

Casual afternoons, after-work drinks

RESERVATIONS

Usually walk-in friendly

INSIDER TIP

Bring your dog—the patio is pet-friendly. The s'mores kit is a fun move.

525 E Menomonee St, Milwaukee, WI 53202

The Rooftop at The Cambria Hotel

$$Downtown

Hotel bar, relaxed, accessible

Hotel rooftop bar with solid views and accessible vibes. Less scene-y than some options, making it a good choice for a relaxed evening drink.

THE VIEW

Downtown views

BEST TIME

Evening drinks

RESERVATIONS

Usually not needed

INSIDER TIP

Good option when other rooftops are packed.

503 N Plankinton Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53203

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best rooftop bars in Milwaukee?

Top Milwaukee rooftop bars include The Outsider at Kimpton Journeyman Hotel (best overall views), Skyline Bar + Lounge at Nō Studios (360° views), Café Benelux/Lux Skyhaus (retractable roof), and Uncle Buck's near Fiserv Forum (best for game days).

Are Milwaukee rooftop bars open year-round?

Some are! The Outsider and Café Benelux's Lux Skyhaus have heated/enclosed areas that operate year-round. Most seasonal rooftops open in late spring and close in early fall. Call ahead to confirm seasonal hours.

Do I need reservations for Milwaukee rooftop bars?

Reservations are recommended for popular spots like The Outsider, Café Benelux, and Skyline Bar, especially on weekends and during sunset hours. More casual spots like Camp Bar and Uncle Buck's are usually walk-in friendly.

Where can I watch Bucks games at a rooftop bar?

Uncle Buck's in the Deer District is the premier rooftop for Bucks games with views of Fiserv Forum and the plaza below. Pilot Project in the nearby Pabst Brewery District is another great pre-game option.

Which Milwaukee rooftop bar has the best views?

The Outsider at Kimpton Journeyman Hotel (9 floors up with 360° views) and Skyline Bar + Lounge at Nō Studios are considered to have the best panoramic city views. Café Benelux offers great Third Ward streetscape views.

Are there any dog-friendly rooftop bars in Milwaukee?

Camp Bar in the Third Ward has a dog-friendly rooftop patio. Always call ahead to confirm current pet policies, as they can change seasonally.
