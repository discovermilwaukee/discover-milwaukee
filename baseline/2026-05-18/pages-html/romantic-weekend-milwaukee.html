COUPLES GUIDE

A Romantic Milwaukee Weekend

Milwaukee's lakefront sunsets, intimate cocktail lounges, and world-class restaurants make it perfect for a couples getaway. Here's how to fall in love with MKE together.

Romantic Restaurants

Milwaukee's dining scene punches well above its weight. These are the most romantic tables in the city.

Sanford

$$$$

Contemporary American

Milwaukee's most celebrated fine dining restaurant. Chef Justin Aprahamian creates seasonal tasting menus that have earned national acclaim. Intimate, elegant, unforgettable.

INSIDER TIP

The 5-course tasting menu is the way to go. Book 2+ weeks ahead.

1547 N Jackson St

Bacchus

$$$$

American Fine Dining

Upscale dining in a gorgeous Cudahy Tower space with stunning views. The wine list is exceptional, and the seasonal menu emphasizes local ingredients.

INSIDER TIP

Request a window table for lakefront views at sunset.

925 E Wells St

Lake Park Bistro

$$$

French

Classic French bistro in a stunning Lake Park pavilion. The terrace overlooks Lake Michigan—arguably the most romantic setting in Milwaukee.

INSIDER TIP

Summer terrace reservations fill up fast—book early.

3133 E Newberry Blvd

EsterEv

$$$$

Tasting Menu

Hidden behind Odd Duck, this intimate 20-seat restaurant serves a nightly tasting menu in a communal setting. Unique and memorable.

INSIDER TIP

Only open Thu-Sat. Reservations required.

2352 S Kinnickinnic Ave

Ardent

$$$$

New American Tasting

Chef Justin Carlisle's intimate tasting menu experience. Creative, seasonal, and intensely focused on craft. Only 24 seats.

INSIDER TIP

The full tasting menu is worth the splurge.

1751 N Farwell Ave

Couples Activities

Beyond dinner—the experiences that make a trip memorable.

Milwaukee Art Museum

Arts & Culture

The Calatrava-designed building is stunning, and the wings opening at 10am is magical. Walk the galleries together, then stroll the lakefront.

MOST ROMANTIC

Watching the Burke Brise Soleil wings open together.

Lakefront Sunset Walk

Outdoor

Walk along the lakefront from Veterans Park to Bradford Beach. The sunset over Lake Michigan is spectacular. Bring a blanket and a bottle of wine.

MOST ROMANTIC

Sunset picnic at Lakeshore State Park with skyline views.

Milwaukee Boat Line Cruise

Experience

River cruises through downtown and the harbor. The sunset and cocktail cruises are particularly romantic.

MOST ROMANTIC

Sunset cruise with champagne and city lights.

Pfister Hotel Art Collection

Arts & Culture

The historic Pfister has a world-class Victorian art collection you can tour for free. End with cocktails at Blu rooftop bar.

MOST ROMANTIC

Cocktails at Blu with panoramic city views.

North Point Lighthouse

Historic

Climb the historic lighthouse for stunning views of the lake and city. Then explore Lake Park's romantic trails.

MOST ROMANTIC

The view from the top at golden hour.

Date Night Bars

Milwaukee's cocktail scene is outstanding. These spots set the mood.

Bryant's Cocktail Lounge

Classic Cocktails

Milwaukee's oldest cocktail lounge with 1930s ambiance. No menu—tell the bartender what you like. Cash only, intimate, timeless.

Vibe: Speakeasy romance

At Random

Tiki/Ice Cream Drinks

Retro supper club vibes with creative ice cream cocktails. The pink neon glow and vinyl booths feel like a date from another era.

Vibe: Retro romantic

Blu at the Pfister

Rooftop Lounge

Swanky rooftop bar atop Milwaukee's most historic hotel. Craft cocktails with panoramic views of the city and lake.

Vibe: Upscale with a view

The Outsider

Rooftop

Kimpton's rooftop bar with craft cocktails and one of the best views in Milwaukee. Perfect for a nightcap.

Vibe: Modern rooftop

Goodkind

Wine Bar

Bay View's beloved wine bar attached to a fantastic restaurant. Intimate, excellent wine list, perfect for a quiet evening.

Vibe: Neighborhood wine bar

Romantic Hotels

The Pfister Hotel

$$$$

Historic Luxury

Milwaukee's grand Victorian hotel. Victorian art collection, Blu rooftop bar, and old-world elegance. Many couples get engaged here.

Iron Horse Hotel

$$$

Boutique Industrial

Industrial-chic in Walker's Point. The loft suites with exposed brick and luxury bedding are incredibly romantic.

Kinn Guesthouse

$$

Boutique B&B

Bay View's coolest boutique stay. Intimate, design-forward rooms above a great restaurant. Very European.

Saint Kate Arts Hotel

$$$

Arts Hotel

Milwaukee's arts-focused hotel with rotating exhibitions, a theater, and stylish rooms. Creative date night built in.
