Best Overall Late Night

The most reliable spots for quality food after hours. These places have earned their late-night reputations.

Gyro Palace

$Walker's PointGreek & Mediterranean

Gyro Palace is Milwaukee's go-to late-night destination for authentic Greek food with a convenient drive-thru window. Their gyros are perfectly seasoned and piled high with fresh toppings, making them the ideal post-bar meal. The Walker's Point location puts you right in the heart of the nightlife district.

MUST TRY

Classic Gyro Sandwich, Chicken Shawarma, Greek Fries

BEST FOR

Post-bar cravings, drive-thru convenience, gyro lovers

Hours: Sun-Thu until 2:30am, Fri-Sat until 3:30am

Pro tip: The drive-thru is a lifesaver after midnight. Order extra tzatziki sauce - you'll want it.

602 S 2nd St, Milwaukee, WI 53204

Oakland Gyros

$East SideGreek Fast Casual

Oakland Gyros has been feeding Milwaukee's East Side late-night crowd for decades. Their massive gyro sandwiches and authentic Greek plates have earned legendary status among UWM students and night owls alike. The no-frills atmosphere is part of the charm.

MUST TRY

Super Gyro, Falafel Plate, Baklava

BEST FOR

East Side crowds, college students, authentic Greek food

Hours: Mon-Thu until 1am, Fri until 2:30am, Sat until 4am

Pro tip: Order the Super Gyro if you're really hungry - it's enormous and worth every penny.

2867 N Oakland Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53211

The Wicked Hop

The Wicked Hop offers upscale pub fare in Milwaukee's trendy Third Ward, staying open late for those who want quality food after hours. Their diverse menu ranges from gourmet burgers to late-night breakfast items, all prepared with care even in the wee hours.

MUST TRY

Crispy Chicken Sandwich, Loaded Cheese Curds, Bavarian Pretzel

BEST FOR

Third Ward nightlife, quality late-night dining, brunch vibes at night

Hours: Open until 2am daily

Pro tip: Their late-night menu kicks in after 10pm with perfect bar food options. The patio is great in summer.

345 N Broadway, Milwaukee, WI 53202

24-Hour Diners

True round-the-clock dining for any hour. These spots never close and always deliver comfort food.

George Webb

$Multiple LocationsClassic Wisconsin Diner

George Webb is a Wisconsin institution, serving breakfast 24 hours a day since 1948. The downtown Old World Third Street location is the perfect late-night stop for pancakes, hash browns, and classic diner fare. No frills, just reliable comfort food whenever you need it.

MUST TRY

Pancakes, Hash Browns, Breakfast Platter

BEST FOR

24-hour breakfast, Wisconsin tradition, reliable and affordable

Hours: Open 24 hours daily

Pro tip: The promise of free burgers if the Brewers win 12 straight is a Milwaukee legend. Downtown location is most convenient.

812 N Old World 3rd St, Milwaukee, WI 53203

Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants are open 24 hours in Milwaukee?

Milwaukee has several true 24-hour dining options including George Webb (multiple locations), Omega Restaurant (Paul's Omega) on S 27th Street, Taqueria La Brazita on the South Side, and Denny's locations. These spots serve food around the clock, perfect for any late-night or early-morning craving.

Where can I get late-night pizza delivery in Milwaukee?

For late-night pizza delivery, Toppers Pizza delivers until 4am on weekends at some locations. Pizza Shuttle delivers until 3am daily, Zayna's Pizza delivers until 3am on weekends, and Ian's Pizza offers delivery until 2am at their downtown location. These are your best bets for pizza delivered after midnight.

What are the best after-bar food spots in Milwaukee?

Top after-bar food destinations include Gyro Palace in Walker's Point (open until 3:30am weekends with drive-thru), The Dogg Haus on Brady Street (gourmet hot dogs until 3am weekends), Oakland Gyros on the East Side (open until 4am Saturdays), and Taqueria La Brazita (24 hours). These spots are strategically located near Milwaukee's nightlife districts.

Where can I find late-night food downtown Milwaukee?

Downtown Milwaukee late-night options include The Wicked Hop in the Third Ward (until 2am), Milwaukee Brat House on Old World Third (kitchen until 1am), George Webb downtown (24 hours), and Who's On Third near Fiserv Forum. Ian's Pizza downtown also serves until late on weekends.

What's the latest a restaurant stays open in Milwaukee?

The latest traditional restaurants stay open in Milwaukee is around 3-4am. Toppers Pizza stays open until 4am Thursday-Saturday, Oakland Gyros until 4am on Saturdays, and Gyro Palace until 3:30am on weekends. For true round-the-clock dining, George Webb, Omega Restaurant, and Taqueria La Brazita operate 24 hours.
