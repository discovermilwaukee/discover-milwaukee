NEIGHBORHOOD GUIDE

Downtown Milwaukee

The heart of Milwaukee—home to the Bucks, world-class theaters, German heritage on Old World Third Street, and the energy of a city on the rise.

JUMP TO

Where to Eat|Attractions|Nightlife

The Vibe

Downtown Milwaukee is where the city comes together. Since the Bucks' championship run and the opening of Fiserv Forum, downtown has experienced a renaissance. The Deer District buzzes with energy on game nights, the theater district draws culture seekers, and Old World Third Street keeps Milwaukee's German heritage alive with beer halls and schnitzel. It's business and pleasure, history and progress, all in one walkable area.

Where to Eat & Drink

From German institutions to sports bars to spy-themed adventures, downtown has something for every appetite.

Mader's German Restaurant

$$

German

A Milwaukee institution since 1902. Mader's is the real deal—authentic German cuisine in a stunning Old World setting filled with medieval armor, stained glass, and Bavarian artifacts. The schnitzel and sauerbraten are legendary.

MUST TRY

Wiener Schnitzel, Sauerbraten, German beer selection

INSIDER TIP

The Knight's Bar downstairs has great happy hour deals and fewer crowds.

1041 N Old World 3rd St, Milwaukee, WI 53203

Elsa's on the Park

$$

American

A Cathedral Square institution known for great burgers and an even better scene. The patio overlooking the park is prime real estate during Jazz in the Park. Classic Milwaukee vibes.

MUST TRY

Burgers, Friday fish fry, people watching

INSIDER TIP

Thursday evenings in summer for free Jazz in the Park right outside.

833 N Jefferson St, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Safe House

$$

American

Milwaukee's legendary spy-themed restaurant and bar. You'll need a password to enter (or perform a silly act). The Cold War decor is elaborate, and it's genuinely fun for groups. A must-do for visitors.

MUST TRY

Theme experience, creative cocktails, shareable apps

INSIDER TIP

The password changes—if you don't know it, you'll have to entertain the doorman.

779 N Front St, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Third Coast Provisions

$$$

Seafood

Upscale seafood restaurant in the heart of downtown serving fresh catches and creative preparations. The raw bar is excellent, and the atmosphere strikes the right balance of sophisticated and welcoming.

MUST TRY

Raw bar, fresh fish preparations, craft cocktails

INSIDER TIP

The raw bar happy hour is one of downtown's best deals.

724 N Milwaukee St, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Mo's Irish Pub

$

Pub Fare

The go-to pre and post-game spot for Bucks and Brewers fans. Multiple floors, tons of TVs, and an electric atmosphere on game days. True Milwaukee sports bar energy.

MUST TRY

Game day atmosphere, beer specials, pub grub

INSIDER TIP

Arrive early on Bucks game nights—it gets packed fast.

142 W Wisconsin Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53203

The Pfister Hotel

$$

Historic Hotel (Victorian Lobby)

Milwaukee's grand Victorian hotel features a stunning lobby worth visiting even if you're not staying. The Blu rooftop bar offers great views, and Café at the Pfister serves solid fare. A downtown landmark since 1893.

MUST TRY

Drinks at Blu rooftop, lobby tour, afternoon tea

INSIDER TIP

The lobby's Victorian art collection is free to view—just walk in.

424 E Wisconsin Ave, Milwaukee, WI 53202

Top Attractions

Sports, arts, history—downtown is packed with things to do.

Fiserv Forum

Sports & Entertainment

Home of the Milwaukee Bucks (2021 NBA Champions) and Marquette Golden Eagles. Also hosts major concerts, WWE events, and more. The Deer District outside is always buzzing.

Bucks gamesConcertsDeer District bars

Milwaukee Theatre District

Performing Arts

Centered around the Marcus Performing Arts Center, Milwaukee's theater district hosts Broadway tours, the Milwaukee Symphony, and local productions. The Riverside and Pabst theaters are historic gems.

Broadway showsSymphonyHistoric theaters

Cathedral Square Park

Public Space

Downtown's cultural heart. Home to Jazz in the Park (Thursdays in summer), farmers markets, and community events. Surrounded by restaurants with great patios.

Jazz in the ParkFarmers marketsFestivals

Old World Third Street

Historic District

Milwaukee's German heritage lives on in this historic street. Mader's, the Wisconsin Cheese Mart, and several German beer halls keep the tradition alive.

German restaurantsBeer hallsHistoric buildings

Pere Marquette Park

Public Space

A beautiful riverfront park perfect for a lunchtime stroll. Home to the iconic bronze deer statue and seasonal ice skating rink.

Riverwalk accessIce skating (winter)Events

Nightlife

From rowdy sports bars to rooftop lounges, downtown keeps the party going.

The Deer District

Entertainment District

The plaza surrounding Fiserv Forum has become Milwaukee's go-to gathering spot. Multiple bars and restaurants, outdoor concerts, and electric energy on game nights.

Vibe: High energy, sports fans, concerts

Water Street

Bar District

Milwaukee's legendary bar strip. College crowds, pub crawls, and late-night energy. Not fancy, but always a scene.

Vibe: Young crowd, bar crawls, late night

Blu at the Pfister

Upscale Lounge

Rooftop bar atop the historic Pfister Hotel with stunning city views. Craft cocktails in a sophisticated setting.

Vibe: Upscale, romantic, great views

Milwaukee Ale House

Brewpub

Craft beers brewed on-site in a beautiful riverwalk location. Great patio for people watching.

Vibe: Casual, craft beer focused, riverfront
